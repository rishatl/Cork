Discoverability Settings

This section lets you enable Discoverability, and customize the way it behaves.

Enable discoverability by turning on the Enable Discoverability toggle.

Setting Name Description
Get top packages for Specify a time frame for which top packages will be shown.
Sort top packages by Specify the sorting order of top packages.
Most downloads: Most-downloaded packages at the top.
Fewest downloads: Least-downloaded packages, after a certain threshold, will be at the top.
Random: Top packages will be in a random order.
